UKCA

my handmade plushies are compliant with UK Toy Safety Regulations (EN71-1, EN71-2, EN71-3 tested). all materials comply are tested and safe according to EN71-3.
there are small parts used which are not for children under 3 years or any individuals who have a tendency to place inedible objects in their mouths. take caution.

testing

all materials have their EN71-3 certificate, which may be viewed in the links above.
EN71-1 Mechanics: I have tested and passed the mechanical test including: washing, pulling (9kg), seams, dropping.
EN71-2 Flammability: I have burnt 60mmx200mm samples and they have self extinguished and not melted.
